WHO WE ARE

About
Master Stonemasons

Master Stonemasons is Ireland’s leading supplier of stone masonry labour, products and fitting solutions.

Set up in 2003 we provide quality stonemasonry, stone works and restoration works to private, residential and commercial stone building projects.

WE OFFER

What we can do for you

Consistently deliver experienced stonemasons for the duration of your project.

Our team of project managers, quantity surveyors, engineers and associates will work closely with your team to guarantee pre-emptive and forward planning to ensure that all materials we will be using are scheduled to be on site when needed.

Rapid progress. With our streamlined management system, we aim to hit the ground running and maintain precision and quality right through to the final building.

Communication and transparency. With regular meetings, everyone working on your project, from senior management to administration to the labourer mixing the mortar, is focussed and motivated to excel in their contribution to this project.
